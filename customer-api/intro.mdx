# Customer Service

The Customer Service is responsible for handling customer-related business logic, including registration, authentication, profile updates, and account state management. It exposes RESTful APIs for creating, retrieving, updating, and managing customer entities.Base URL

Key responsibilities include:

- Customer onboarding
- Authentication and authorization integration
- Profile and preference management
- Account activation/deactivation
- Data validation and integrity enforcement
